About The Mango

The Mango is a coastal Mexican restaurant inspired by the tropical atmosphere of Tecomán, Colima. Surrounded by mature mango trees, the restaurant offers fresh seafood, traditional Mexican cuisine, handcrafted cocktails, and an unforgettable outdoor dining experience.
