Welcome to The Mango

The Mango is a relaxed restaurant where guests can enjoy fresh food, refreshing drinks, and a warm atmosphere. Inspired by the flavors of Tecomán, Colima, our goal is to create a place where friends, families, and visitors can come together to enjoy good meals and good company.

Whether you are stopping by for lunch, dinner, or a drink in the evening, The Mango offers a laid-back setting that feels welcoming and comfortable.
